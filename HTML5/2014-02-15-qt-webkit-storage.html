Client Storage

QT 目前支持 WebStorage 和 WebSQL Databases。

While common local- or session-based databases are capable of storing complex data structures, QtWebKit-based browsers can also rely upon the WebSQL standard, which brings SQLite-based structured database functionality, typically deployed on servers, to client browser applications. Based on SQLite version 3.6.19, WebSQL is appropriate for data-intensive applications requring complex queries rather than simple key/value access.
